Role: Lead Designer

This is a card game that is currently in developement by a group of RIT students and myself. The game is currently being developed for our Game Design and Developement 1 course.

This is a fast-paced, reflex based, card game which incorporates several deck-building elements. The game takes place in an old western frontier town and the players are legendary wild west gunfighters. The game will incorporate themes from traditional spaghetti westerns (The Good, The Bad, and The Ugly, Tombstone, A Fistful of Dollars, etc.), Mexican standoffs, dueling, and all sorts of roguish behaviour that would be common in the more unsavoury parts of the old west. The objective of the game is to be the last gunfighter standing by way of shootouts, betrayal, and alliances.
