Role: Lead Designer and Programmer

This was a small class project that I developed in processing. It was my first time implementing physics and steering forces in a game, and I believe it came out very well. The graphics are purposefully minimalistic to focus the player on the game. One of my own twists that I added on to the asteroids formula is that the player has the ability to curve their bullets in the game.
