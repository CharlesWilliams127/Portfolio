Role: Modeller, Rigger, Texturer

This is a Unity3D project that I developed with a group for a 3D modelling class. I was the primary modeller for this project, as well as the environment designer. I also contributed to UV unwrapping, rigging, animating, and texturing each of the models.

For this project we wanted to recreate the feel of a 19th century fur trapper's cabin. We did a lot of research to make this demo, such as what type of wood the cabin would be made out of, what types of materials they would have access to, etc. to bring to the user an authentic and realistic world that they may walk around and observe using a first person controller.
